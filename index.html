Performer, Sound Artist, Creator, Live Electronics, Researcher and Educator.

Mexican artist that has developed a passion for detail in sound. He has performed the most representative works of the tuba soloist repertoire. As a Live Electronics artist he has focused on audio reactivity and sensors, both sound and visual, based on the speech developed by his instrument. Interested in multidisciplinary projects, he has collaborated with different artists and been a lead teacher at the undergraduate level at three different Mexican Institutions. He is currently developing a research in what he defines as "Musical Rubrik". He has performed in Mexico, Canada, Chile, South Korea, Czech Republic, Belgium, Croatia and the Netherlands.
